Ferns United AFC

Why do we need an App?

A dedicated app for our football club is essential to improve communication and convenience for our 500 members. With so many updates about games, training sessions, events, and fundraisers, it’s easy for important information to get lost across social media and messaging platforms.

Step 1: Download the App

Follow the links below to download KlubApp for your device.

Download for iOS Download for Android

Step 2: Setting Up the App

Open KlubApp on your device.


Sign Up or log in with your existing details.



Search for "Ferns United"



Select "Ferns United FC" and follow the prompts to join.



Then Sign in to the App using your email and password


Step 3: Using the App

KlubApp offers a number of services on the App, however for now we are only using Notifications and News Feed

Access menu from here. At the foot of the App you can also access Notifications and News Feed



For now, we are solely utilizing Notifications and News Feed



Notifications will appear as follows on your device:



In the App - when you select "Notifications" you'll see the message



Step 4: KlubApp Notifications

If you do not receive notifications, make sure you enable notifications in the app so you can stay updated with the latest news and events from Ferns United

Go to the app's settings.

Enable notifications for your device.

Choose to receive updates from Ferns United AFC.


Step 5: You're All Set!

You're now ready to receive updates, event details, and important notifications from Ferns United through KlubApp. Thank you for staying connected with us!
